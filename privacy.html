Privacy Policy for Posy Period Tracker

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Posy is a privacy-first period tracker designed with your privacy as the top priority.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All your health data stays on your device only.

Data Storage

  • All cycle data, logs, and settings are stored locally on your device
  • We have no servers that receive your data
  • We cannot access your information

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we don't have access to your data.

Analytics

Posy does not include any analytics or tracking tools.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can export or delete your data at any time from the app settings.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
